Common Pain Points

Recognize any of these?

These are the friction points I see most often in small businesses — and every one of them has a practical fix.

📋
Inventory tracked in someone's head

Nobody knows what's in stock until it runs out. Orders are reactive and rushed. A well-built Excel tracker with auto-reorder flags changes this immediately — you can see every item, every threshold, and every pending order in one place.

🔁
The same task done manually every week

Weekly reports, sign-in sheets, order summaries — done by hand, every time. A VBA macro can collapse hours of repetitive work into a single button click, pulling data, formatting it, and outputting a clean report automatically.

📊
Data exists but doesn't tell you anything

Sales figures, hours, usage rates — it's all there in a spreadsheet somewhere, but no one's looked at it in months because it's just a wall of numbers. Pivot tables and summary views turn that raw data into something your team can actually read and act on.

🚪
Knowledge walks out when people leave

When a long-term employee moves on, their process goes with them. Without written job aids or SOPs, you're back to square one every time. I document those processes clearly so any new hire can be up to speed from day one.

📁
Forms and documents scattered everywhere

Intake forms in email, checklists printed and re-printed, sign-in logs in a binder nobody touches. Fillable PDFs and standardized Word templates bring everything into one consistent format that's easy to complete, store, and find.

💸
Expensive software that only solves half the problem

Monthly subscriptions that require training, setup, and ongoing IT support — for problems that a properly built spreadsheet could solve for free. Most small businesses already have everything they need in Excel and Word.

VBA Macros & Automation

Custom VBA macros that automate repetitive tasks — generating weekly reports, formatting imported data, sending summary emails, or consolidating entries from multiple sheets. What used to take an hour can take seconds.

Excel Pivot Reports & Data Analysis

Pivot tables, charts, and summary dashboards that pull meaning out of data you're already collecting. Sales by category, hours by employee, usage by department — presented clearly so decisions get made faster.

Word / PDF Forms & Documents

Fillable PDF forms, standardized intake documents, sign-in logs, inspection checklists, and any other paper-based process that needs to be clean, consistent, and professional. Print-ready or digital — your choice.

Word / PDF Job Aids & SOPs

Step-by-step procedure guides, visual job aids, and standard operating procedures that capture exactly how things get done in your business. Written clearly enough that a brand-new hire can follow them without hand-holding.

My Process

From first conversation to working solution

A straightforward engagement built around your timeline — no long retainers, no mystery pricing.

STEP 01

Talk It Through

We start with a conversation — no forms, no commitment. You describe where things feel slow, broken, or manual. I ask questions about your workflow, your team size, and how you currently handle the problem. The conversation is always free.

STEP 02

Scope the Solution

Once I understand the problem, we discuss what level of solution actually makes sense — simple template, automated workbook, or something more involved. I may need brief access to a sample of your data or a look at your current setup to scope it accurately. You get a flat-rate written quote before anything starts.

STEP 03

Design, Build & Test

I build the solution, test it against real scenarios, and refine it until it handles your actual data and edge cases — not just a clean example. You'll see a working version early so we can adjust before anything is finalized.

STEP 04

Implement, Train & Stay Available

We roll it out together. I walk your team through how to use it, make sure it's plugged into your actual workflow, and leave with full documentation. You own everything completely — the files, the logic, the process. And if something comes up down the road, troubleshooting support is always available.
